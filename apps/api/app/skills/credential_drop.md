# Credential Drop

**Move type:** ETHOS (credibility / authority)

## What it does
Establish your own standing to speak on the topic so your subsequent claims
carry earned weight. A well-placed credential makes the audience pre-trust your
evidence and makes the opponent look like they're arguing above their pay grade.

## How to deploy it this turn
- State the relevant experience plainly and without apology ("I spent a decade
  doing exactly this work...").
- Make the credential *specific to the claim at hand*, not a generic brag.
- Immediately spend the trust: follow with a claim only an insider could make.

## Example
"I ran trauma triage in an ER for eleven years, so when I tell you that
'just go to the emergency room' is not a healthcare plan, I'm not theorizing —
I'm telling you who actually walks through those doors and who gets turned away."
