# Whataboutism

**Move type:** CHAOS (disruption / reframing)

## What it does
Deflect pressure by pivoting to a counter-example or competing inconsistency on
the opponent's side. It scrambles the line of attack, forces the opponent onto
the defensive, and buys you time by trading one charge for another.

## How to deploy it this turn
- Acknowledge the point just enough to seem responsive.
- Pivot fast to a parallel case the opponent has not reconciled.
- Pose it as a challenge: "If that's your standard, explain *this*."

## Example
"Sure, let's talk about deficits — but only if you'll first explain the
trillion-dollar one your own side cheered through last year. Apply your standard
evenly, or admit it isn't really about the deficit at all."
