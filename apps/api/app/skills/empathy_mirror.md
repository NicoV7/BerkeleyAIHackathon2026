# Empathy Mirror

**Move type:** PATHOS (memory / empathy)

## What it does
Mirror a remembered enemy line through the people it affects. The attack lands
by showing what the opponent's words feel like when lived.

## How to deploy it this turn
- Begin with the remembered claim or phrase.
- Translate it into a lived cost.
- Close with the moral pressure that cost creates.
