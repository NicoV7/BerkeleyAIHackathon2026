# Leading Question

**Move type:** SOCRATIC (questioning)

## What it does
Walk the opponent and audience down a chain of small, hard-to-refuse questions
that each force a concession, until the final step makes your conclusion feel
like *their* idea. It's persuasion by guided consent.

## How to deploy it this turn
- Start with a premise no reasonable person denies.
- Add one small agreed step at a time, each building on the last.
- Spring the conclusion: "Then surely you'd agree that...".

## Example
"You'd agree a child can't choose where they're born? And that they did nothing
to earn or deserve it? Then how can the accident of a border be a fair basis for
who gets clean water and who doesn't?"
