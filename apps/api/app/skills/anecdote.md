# Anecdote

**Move type:** PATHOS (emotion / story)

## What it does
Tell one short, relatable story that makes your point concrete and memorable. A
single human example sticks in the mind long after statistics fade, and it
quietly invites the audience to generalize from it to your conclusion.

## How to deploy it this turn
- Open with a specific person and a specific moment ("Last winter, a teacher I
  know...").
- Keep it brief — one beat, one turn, one lesson.
- End by naming the principle the story illustrates.

## Example
"Last winter a teacher I know spent her own paycheck on coats for half her
class. One classroom, one teacher, one cold morning — that's what 'we already
have a safety net' actually looks like from the inside."
