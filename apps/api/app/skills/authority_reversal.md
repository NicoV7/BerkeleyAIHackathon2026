# Authority Reversal

**Move type:** ETHOS (memory / authority)

## What it does
Use remembered claims and the agent's persona authority to reverse the burden:
the opponent must justify why their own record should be trusted.

## How to deploy it this turn
- Cite the remembered claim as evidence of their standard.
- Speak from the agent's persona, expertise, or earned stance.
- Force the opponent to defend their credibility before their conclusion.
