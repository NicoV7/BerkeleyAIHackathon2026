# Steel Man

**Move type:** LOGOS (logic / evidence)

## What it does
Restate the opponent's argument in its strongest possible form — stronger than
they made it — and then defeat *that* version. Demolishing the best case proves
your position survives even the toughest opposition, and it strips the opponent
of any "you misunderstood me" escape.

## How to deploy it this turn
- Summarize the opponent's point generously and accurately: "The strongest
  version of your argument is...".
- Concede whatever is genuinely true in it.
- Then show that even at full strength it fails against your evidence or logic.

## Example
"The best version of your case is that markets self-correct faster than
regulators can act — and historically that's often true. But in 2008 the
self-correction was a global depression averted only by intervention. Even
granting your strongest premise, the conclusion collapses."
