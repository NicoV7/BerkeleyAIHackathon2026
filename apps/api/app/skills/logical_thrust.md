# Logical Thrust

**Move type:** LOGOS (logic / evidence)

## What it does
Drive a single, well-sourced fact or tight chain of reasoning straight at the
weakest joint of the opponent's argument. This is a clean offensive jab: one
concrete claim, one inference, one conclusion the opponent cannot dodge.

## How to deploy it this turn
- Lead with a specific, verifiable fact, statistic, or causal mechanism.
- State the inference explicitly: "Because X, it follows that Y."
- End by naming what the opponent's position must now explain or concede.
- Keep it sharp. One thrust lands harder than five vague gestures.

## Example
"Emissions fell 12% the year the carbon fee took effect, while GDP rose 3%.
That single pairing dismantles the claim that pricing carbon wrecks the economy
— so the burden is now on you to explain that data away."
