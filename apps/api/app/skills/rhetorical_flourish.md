# Rhetorical Flourish

**Move type:** RHETORIC (style / framing)

## What it does
Deploy a memorable turn of phrase — a tricolon, an antithesis, a callback — that
makes your point *stick*. Style is not decoration: a line the audience can
repeat is a line they'll carry into the verdict.

## How to deploy it this turn
- Compress your strongest point into one rhythmic, balanced sentence.
- Use a device: rule of three, contrast, or repetition.
- Land it last so it's the line that echoes after you stop talking.

## Example
"We were promised a ladder and handed a treadmill: more effort, same place,
and someone up top insisting we simply aren't running hard enough."
