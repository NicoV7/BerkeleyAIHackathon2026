# Socratic Probe

**Move type:** SOCRATIC (questioning)

## What it does
Ask one precise question that exposes a gap, contradiction, or unexamined
assumption in the opponent's position. Rather than asserting they're wrong, you
make *them* surface the flaw — which is far harder to dismiss than your own
accusation.

## How to deploy it this turn
- Pick the single load-bearing assumption you want to test.
- Frame a clear, answerable question with no easy escape.
- Leave the silence. The unanswered question *is* the argument.

## Example
"You say the program creates dependency. So let me ask directly: what's your
evidence that recipients work less — and if the data shows they work *more*,
will you withdraw the claim?"
